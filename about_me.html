Ryan Adra

I am a current undergraduate student at University of California, Irvine majoring in Computer Science with an Information Specialization. My passion in technology started back in 8th grade when I wanted to create the next social media network because meeting the online communities I made there has developed me into the person I am today. Now, I want to use technology, specifically UI/UX design, for social good. I am interested in creating inclusive and accessible experiences with the designs I create, hoping to help communities I'm passionate about, including Pilipinx-Americans, first generation immigrants, mental health advocates, and more.

Some of my interests include socks with sandals, Trader Joe's snacks, and bogo deals. On the weekends, you will see me either catching up with the latest episode of my show, trying a new food place, or buying secondhand clothing. Click on the photos to find out more about me.
